CALCULATOR

  • Body Mass Index (BMI): BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²
  • Creatinine Clearance (Cockcroft-Gault): CrCl = [(140 - age) × weight (kg)] / [72 × serum Cr (mg/dL)] (×0.85 for females)
  • GFR (MDRD): GFR = 175 × (serum Cr)^-1.154 × (age)^-0.203 (×0.742 for females, ×1.212 for African Americans)
  • Estimated Blood Volume: EBV = weight (kg) × average blood volume (mL/kg)
  • CT Dose Index (CTDI): CTDI = D × L
  • Radiation Dose (Effective): Effective Dose = DLP × k
  • Renal Function (eGFR): eGFR = 186 × (serum Cr)^-1.154 × (age)^-0.203 (×0.742 for females)
  • Corrected Calcium: Corrected Ca = measured Ca + 0.8 × (4 - albumin)
  • Alveolar-arterial Gradient: A–a gradient = PAO₂ – PaO₂
  • Osmolality: Osmolality = 2 × Na + glucose/18 + BUN/2.8
